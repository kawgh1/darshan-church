"Feed My sheep."



John 21:17

Darshan Church has only recently obtained an online presence. For a generation we have preached the Gospel of Jesus Christ in every foothill and village around Darshan, providing rice, goats, clothing, bedding, supplies, water and well systems, etc. to all walks of life. We ask for your help to continue to spread the Good News of Jesus and alleviate the suffering of the people here in Nepal.

Currently, Nepal is not approved for international online bank transactions such as PayPal and Stripe. Western Union is currently our only option.

At this time, if you choose to support us through Western Union directly, you will need to contact Sujan Ghimire at vision320ch@gmail.com so that she can receive the funds locally here in Nepal. Sujan is quick to respond, fluent in English and is happy to send pictures of receipts or items purchased with your donations.

For $100 USD we can feed 4 families for a month each.



We give to the most needy and vulnerable first - single mothers, widows, elderly, disabled, etc.

From the bottom of our hearts, we thank each and every one of you around the world!



Your prayers, your encouragement, your "likes" and comments are all a blessing to us here at Darshan.



Pray for us as we pray for you and for all people of God.
